My Design Process

Step 1: Product design Research

I take the time to understand clients and their vision to help bring their product to life. Using the design thinking process or the Double Diamond framework, I refine ideas through iteration and collaborate with professionals to generate meaningful insights that drive the project forward.

Step 2: UI design and prototyping in figma

Proficient in Tailwind’s style guidelines, I’m adaptable and comfortable working with various design frameworks. I understand when to use rough prototypes and wireframes for early exploration and when to refine designs with pixel-perfect precision for developer handoff.

Step 3: No-code dev. or collaborative dev

I have a strong independent skill set and hands-on experience with various CMS platforms, starting with WordPress before transitioning to Wix Studio. At the same time, I’m a collaborative team player who understands IT terminology and seamlessly connects with developers and coding professionals.
